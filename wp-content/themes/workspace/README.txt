Workspace WordPress Theme 1.0
http://www.theme-junkie.com/themes/workspace

INSTALL: 
1. Upload the theme folder /workspace via FTP to your wp-content/themes/ directory.
2. Go to your WordPress dashboard and activate the Workspace theme.
3. Inside your WordPress dashboard, go to Appearance > Workspace Theme Options and configure them to your liking.
